How To Play

Welcome to jPs Generic Snake Game! To begin click start game. Your snake will begin 3 pieces long, as you collect food your tail will grow anywhere from 1 - 3 pieces depending on the food you eat. You will also gain points based on the food you eat. Each tier of food will be available for a limited time based on how rare the food is.

  • Bronze food is worth 10pts, adds 1 segment, and lasts the longest
  • Silver food is worth 20pts, adds 2 segment, and lasts a little less time than Bronze
  • Gold food is worth 30pts, adds 3 segment, but dissapears VERY quick

Control your snake using the arrow keys (important: PRESS, DO NOT HOLD THE KEYS) on your keyboard. The game is over when you run into the wall or your own table!
